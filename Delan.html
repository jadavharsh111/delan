WHAT WE ARE

We at Delan pioneer to bring the local shops on the e-commerce map.

In the era of increasing ecommerce dependence of the customers in grocery and other daily use commodities,
the offline retailers bear a loss. This happens because the customer has a pool of services at his disposal
which include certain information about the product like its price, delivery time and others. To rectify this and
to bridge the gap between online and offline stores providing benefits to the retailers as well as the customers,
Delan comes to the fore.
